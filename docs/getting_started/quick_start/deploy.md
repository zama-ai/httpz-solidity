## Interacting with the smart contract

#### Interacting with your smart contract

Check under deployed contracts:

- you will see orange and blue boxes that include:
  - acceptOwnership
  - ...
  - symbol
  - totalSupply
- Copy the Address
- Go to ZAMA plugin -> paste the address you have copied under DEPLOY
- You should again see orange and blue boxes, however these are equiped with encryption and decryption functionalities

#### Mint tokens

- Go to mint box
- Copy your address: paste in the to box and paste in the amount of 10000, press submit
- pop up for wallet
